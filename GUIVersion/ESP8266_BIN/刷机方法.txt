连线方法
7pin SPI引脚，正面看，从左到右依次为GND、VCC、D0、D1、RES、DC、CS
   ESP8266 ---  OLED
     3V    ---  VCC
     G     ---  GND
     D7    ---  D1
     D5    ---  D0
     D2orD8---  CS
     D1    ---  DC
     RST   ---  RES
4pin IIC引脚，正面看，从左到右依次为GND、VCC、SCL、SDA
     OLED  ---  ESP8266
     VCC   ---  3.3V
     GND   ---  G (GND)
     SCL   ---  D1(GPIO5)
     SDA   ---  D2(GPIO4)
*/
